Getting Here

Easy to find on Highway 101, right in the heart of Depoe Bay.

From Portland (approx. 2 hours)

Take I-5 South to Salem, then OR-22 West to OR-18 West, then US-101 South to Depoe Bay.

From Eugene (approx. 2.5 hours)

Take OR-126 West to Florence, then US-101 North to Depoe Bay.

From Lincoln City (approx. 15 min)

Head south on US-101. The salon is on the right as you enter Depoe Bay.

From Newport (approx. 15 min)

Head north on US-101. The salon is on the left just past the Depoe Bay Bridge.

Free parking available right in front of the salon on Highway 101. Easy pull-in access with no meters or time limits.

Discover Depoe Bay

Known as the Whale Watching Capital of the Oregon Coast, Depoe Bay is a charming coastal village nestled along Highway 101 between Lincoln City and Newport. The town sits atop dramatic basalt cliffs, offering some of the most breathtaking ocean views on the Pacific seaboard.

Step outside the salon and you are moments from iconic landmarks: the historic Depoe Bay Bridge spanning the world's smallest navigable harbor, the famous Spouting Horn where ocean swells blast skyward through rocky channels, and a fleet of whale watching charters that depart daily in search of resident gray whales.

We are just minutes from Lincoln City, Newport, Otter Rock, and the Central Oregon Coast's best beaches, tide pools, and seafood restaurants. Make a day of it: get your nails done at The Nail Ladie, then explore the coast. Grab chowder at a waterfront cafe, browse the galleries in Nye Beach, or watch the sunset from Boiler Bay State Scenic Viewpoint just up the road.
