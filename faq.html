Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know about nail services, lashes, hair, and visiting our Depoe Bay salon.

General & Location

Where is The Nail Ladie located?

We're at 4040 N Highway 101, Depoe Bay, OR 97341 — right on the Oregon Coast. Easy to reach from Lincoln City, Newport, Siletz, Toledo, and the greater Central Coast area. Look for us on the highway just north of the Depoe Bay Bridge.

What are your hours of operation?

Wednesday through Saturday, 8:00 AM to 6:45 PM. We're closed Sunday through Tuesday. We recommend booking ahead since Heather works one-on-one with each client.

Is there parking available?

Yes — free parking is available right in front of the salon on Highway 101. There's plenty of room for easy pull-in access, even for larger vehicles.

Is The Nail Ladie a private salon?

Absolutely. This is a one-chair, one-client salon. You'll have Heather's complete and undivided attention for your entire appointment. No other clients, no rush, no noise — just a quiet, personalized experience.

How long has Heather been doing nails?

Heather brings over 18 years of professional experience in nails, lashes, and beauty. She's a licensed cosmetologist who has worked with thousands of clients across Oregon.

Do you accept walk-ins?

Because this is a private, one-on-one salon, appointments are strongly recommended. Walk-ins may be accommodated if there's availability, but booking in advance ensures you get the time slot and service you want.

Yes! Many clients combine nails with lash services or hair. When booking online, select all the services you'd like and the system will block the appropriate time. You can also call to coordinate a multi-service appointment.

Nail Services & Pricing

What nail services do you offer?

We offer a full range: gel manicures ($45), gel full sets ($75), gel fills ($55), dip powder manicures ($55), basic manicures ($30), nail art (from $10), gel removal ($15), and nail repairs ($10). We also have premium add-ons like chrome, cat eye, rhinestone art, French tip upgrades, and paraffin wax treatments.

What's the difference between gel and dip powder?

Gel nails are cured under UV/LED light and offer a glossy, flexible finish that lasts 2–3 weeks. Dip powder involves dipping nails into colored powder with a bonding agent — it tends to be slightly more durable and doesn't require UV curing. Both look beautiful; Heather can help you choose what's best for your lifestyle.

How long do gel nails last?

Gel manicures typically last 2–3 weeks with proper care. Gel full sets and fills can last 3–4 weeks before a fill is needed. Avoiding harsh chemicals, wearing gloves for cleaning, and using cuticle oil daily will help extend the life of your manicure.

Do you do nail art?

Yes — Heather is known for her nail art. Options include hand-painted designs, chrome and cat eye finishes, rhinestone and gem placement, French tip variations, ombre, marble, and seasonal designs. Nail art starts at $10 per nail depending on complexity. Bring your inspiration photos!

Can I see examples of your nail work?

Check out our gallery or follow @the_nail_ladie on Instagram for the latest designs and client photos.

How often should I get a fill?

Most clients schedule fills every 2–3 weeks, depending on how fast their nails grow. Waiting too long between fills can cause lifting, which increases the risk of water or bacteria getting under the nail. We'll advise you on the ideal schedule for your nails.

Eyelash Extensions

How much do eyelash extensions cost?

Classic lash full sets start at $150, volume lash full sets at $200. Lash fills are $75 (classic) and $100 (volume). We also offer lash lift & tint for $85 and lash removal for $25.

What's the difference between classic and volume lashes?

Classic lashes involve applying one extension per natural lash for a natural, elegant look. Volume lashes use ultra-fine fans of 2–6 extensions per lash for a fuller, more dramatic result. Heather will recommend the best style for your eye shape and desired look.

How long do lash extensions last?

Lash extensions last through the natural lash growth cycle, typically 4–6 weeks. Most clients schedule fills every 2–3 weeks to keep them looking full. Proper aftercare — avoiding oil-based products and not rubbing your eyes — extends their life significantly.

What is a lash lift and tint?

A lash lift curls your natural lashes from the base, giving them a lifted, wide-open look — like a perm for your lashes. The tint darkens them so you look like you're wearing mascara without any product. Results last 6–8 weeks. It's $85 and a great low-maintenance option.
