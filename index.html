Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Where is The Nail Ladie located?

The Nail Ladie is located at 4040 N Highway 101, Depoe Bay, OR 97341, on the beautiful Oregon Coast. We're easily accessible from Lincoln City, Newport, and the greater Central Coast area.

What are your hours?

We're open Wednesday through Saturday, 8:00 AM to 6:45 PM. Closed Sunday through Tuesday. We recommend booking in advance as Heather works one-on-one with each client.

How do I book an appointment?

You can book online anytime at vagaro.com/thenailladie or call us at (541) 992-1887.

What nail services do you offer?

We offer gel manicures ($45), gel full sets ($75), gel fills ($55), dip powder ($55), basic manicures ($30), nail art (from $10), gel removal ($15), and nail repairs ($10). Add-ons include chrome/cat eye, rhinestone art, French tip upgrades, and paraffin wax.

How much do eyelash extensions cost?

Classic lash full sets start at $150, volume lash full sets at $200. Fills are $75 (classic) and $100 (volume). We also offer lash lift & tint for $85 and lash removal for $25.

Is this a private salon?

Yes — The Nail Ladie is an intimate, one-chair salon. You receive Heather's undivided attention for your entire appointment. No rush, no noise, no other clients. It's a truly personalized experience.
